Frequenctly Asked Questions

Why can't i select seats by leaving a seat in between?  

The purpose is to optimize seating arrangements in the cinema hall so that patrons in group will not have to be separated especially when the occupancy is high.

What should I do if I lose my tickets or make a wrong booking?  

Please proceed to the booking cinema at least 1 hour before the movie session to seek assistance from staff. Alternatively, if the movie haven't start yet you may email our Customer Service team (we will response to you within 4 working days) at customersvc@sawcinematics.com or call 1-800-8700-7417 (during Customer Service Operating Hours) for further assistance.

What do the different film classifications mean? (G, PG, PG13, NC16, M18 and R21) ?  

Films in Singapore are rated according to the following ratings
General (G) Suitable for all ages.
Parental Guidance (PG) Suitable for all but parents should guide their young.
Parental Guidance 13 (PG13) Suitable for persons aged 13 and above but parental guidance is advised for children below 13.
No Children under 16 (NC16) Restricted to persons aged 16 and above.
Mature 18 (M18) Restricted to persons aged 18 and above.
Restricted 21 (R21) Restricted to persons aged 21 and above.
